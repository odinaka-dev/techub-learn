Frequently asked questions

what is tech hub?

Tech hub is an online documentation platform that provides new or existing (but not satisfied) techies with the basic documents required for them to study and become massive techies. It tracks techies progress as it helps them to navigate their progress through a detailed road map brochure.

How do I get started?

Log-into our website and pick any available course (web based courses) and start reading through the process. you can select any and every documentation. All resources are free and accessible to all users of the website.

Are the resources Free?

All resources are free and accessible to all users of the website. Every documents on this website are open and free to all. Your commitment to learn is all that is needed.

Is there a certificate upon completion?

There is no certificate upon completion of the courses on this website. However you can share what you learn online as well as solve problems with the skill you will possess upon completion of the course on this website.

Is it a lifetime access?

Yes, it is a lifetime access.
